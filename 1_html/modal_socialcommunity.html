Social Community

Social communication is a mutual exchange between actors of semantic and evaluative socially significant information that affects both the actors of social interaction and society as a whole.

The transfer of information during the communication process requires certain media - symbols and signs, since communication is by its nature a symbolic process. Symbols and signs are objects that reveal the meaning that they do not contain on their own, but due to the fact that society has endowed them with this meaning. That meaning is the meaning of the sign. Therefore, we can say that communication is a process of deciphering signs and reading their social meanings.

We use the most popular social web products

Signs are words, gestures, poses, facial expressions, sometimes expressive behavior (laughter, smiles, tears, sighs, etc.).

Language is the most important symbolic system institutionalized by society and therefore having a historical character. An individual is born and formed in a specific social context where a specific language structure has already been set. Therefore, the language of any society indirectly reflects the structure of society itself and, as it were, sets the specifics of social communication.
